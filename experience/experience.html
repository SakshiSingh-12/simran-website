4. Masters Dissertation

Masters Dissertation: “Construction of Balanced cross-section & Estimation of Horizontal Shortening across Tripura Folds with focus on its gas potential". work presented in conference: XXVII INDIAN COLLOQUIUM ON MICROPALEONTOLOGY AND STRATIGRAPHY 2019.
• Nov 6, 2019
• Mentor- Prof. Jokhan Ram (Department of Geology, BHU; Ex Exploration Director, KDMIPE (Keshava Deva Malviya Institute of Petroleum Exploration), Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited, Dehradun). Description- This research paper aims to study structural style, estimation of shortening and comparison of western, central, eastern Tripura Fold Belt and the loci of stress partitioning in the area. A well constrained east –west oriented regional geological transect had been constructed across the fold belt. While the study makes a significant academic contribution to the understanding of the Tripura-Cachar-Chittongang fold belt, the outcome of the study is expected to provide renewed focus on new plays and new fairways.
